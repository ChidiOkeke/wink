Drowsy: The Case Study

Project Overview

This project involved designing a poster that effectively utilizes typography and color to create a visually appealing and engaging design.

Design Concept

The poster, titled "Drowsy," combines vibrant colors and bold typography to capture attention. The flowing abstract patterns and striking color contrasts are intended to evoke a sense of motion and energy.

Typography

The choice of typography plays a critical role in the design. The bold, chunky font used for "DROWSY" stands out against the colorful background, while the repeated, semi-transparent text adds depth and a dynamic feel to the composition.

Color Scheme

The color palette includes bright yellows, reds, greens, and blues, creating a lively and energetic vibe. The swirling colors blend seamlessly, creating an abstract background that enhances the bold typography.

Result

The final design successfully achieves its goal of creating a visually striking poster that draws viewers in and holds their attention. The use of typography and color effectively communicates the theme and emotion intended.
